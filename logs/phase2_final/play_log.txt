Key findings
------------
- Tilt-max plateaued at ~0.19 across v4-v7 (four different reward/schedule configs).
  This is a kinematic floor: the 50 kg body must physically pitch to absorb momentum
  during the gait contact-pattern flip. Not policy-trainable. Recovery: <1 s per switch.
- Smoothstep alpha schedule (v7) improved vx tracking (+0.440 vs +0.426 in v4) and
  reduced vx variance (std 0.100 vs 0.115 in v4) -- cleaner steady-state between switches.
- Per-leg delta-alpha magnitude is small (max 0.197 vs 0.8 cap) -- the MLP is operating
  in the fine-correction regime, not heroically compensating for a bad baseline.
- Steady-state delta-alpha ~= 0 (time-gating + sparsity confirmed effective).
